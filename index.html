WHO IS SUGAR-KISSED

SUGAR-KISSED is a charming brand that was established on March 13, 2008, by Christianne Marie Bohol and Maria Iris Ivy Bohol. This dynamic duo embarked on a creative venture to bring a touch of sweetness and style to children's fashion. In its early days, Sugarkissed initially focused on crafting exquisite headbands and hair accessories, adding a dash of whimsy to little ones' hairstyles. As the brand flourished, we expanded its offerings to include made-to-order children's dresses inspired by beloved Disney and other movie characters.We also have our own unique touch of original customize dresses. Every Sugarkissed dress is meticulously tailored with attention to detail, allowing children to immerse themselves in the enchanting world of Disney and other popular children movies while radiating confidence and elegance. With a commitment to quality craftsmanship and imaginative design, Sugarkissed continues to bring joy and magic to the hearts of children and their families. ...
